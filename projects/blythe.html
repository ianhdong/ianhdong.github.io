Blythe Mental Health Hub

  • Organization: Gigahacks
  • Tech: HTML, CSS
  • Project Date: November 2019
  • Project Link: Blythe Mental Health Hub

During my high school's annual hackathon, I wanted to build a website that could help impact society and thus this prototype was born. As a student, I felt a lot of stress and wanted stronger mental health resources so I created a working mental health hub that could connect students with the aid of professionals. Features include an integrated Discord chat bot and gallery with uplifting quotes. I want to continue working on this project and hope to turn it into a working app.
